🌿 About Our Company

From Indian Farms to Global Markets

Ravni Farmers Producer Company Limited is a farmer-led organization based in Gujarat, India, committed to delivering premium-quality agricultural products to domestic and international markets.

We are directly connected with farmers at the grassroots level, ensuring complete control over quality, sourcing, and supply. By eliminating middlemen, we provide competitive pricing while maintaining the highest standards of purity and consistency.

✨ Why Choose Us

Our Strengths & Commitment

We deliver excellence at every step — from sourcing to packaging. Here's what makes us stand out.

Direct from Farmers

We source products directly from farmers, ensuring authenticity, freshness, and fair pricing without middlemen.

Premium Quality Assurance

All products are carefully cleaned, graded, and processed to meet domestic and international quality standards.

Flexible Packaging

We offer customized packaging options including 25kg / 50kg bags and bulk packaging as per buyer requirements.

Export-Oriented Supply

Our systems and processes are designed to serve global markets with consistent quality and timely delivery.

Reliable & Transparent

We believe in long-term relationships through honest communication, clear pricing, and dependable service.

Strong Farmer Network

With a wide network of farmers, we ensure consistent supply and the ability to handle bulk orders efficiently.

🎯 Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Driven by purpose, guided by values — empowering farmers and delivering excellence.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower farmers by connecting them directly to markets while delivering high-quality agricultural products to customers worldwide with trust, transparency, and consistency.

🌍

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized farmer producer company by promoting sustainable agriculture, enhancing farmer income, and establishing a strong presence in international agricultural trade.

"We aim to build a future where farmers grow with pride and customers receive the finest quality directly from the source."
